How Is Humanity to Respond to Climate Change?

And why now is the time for a feminist climate science approach.

Generally there exists two opposing mentalities in how to address the impending climate change crisis: technoscientific and regenerative/restorative. A technoscientific approach that is being proposed and actively pursued is geoengineering. A regenerative/restorative approach that roots itself in the environmental movement is permaculture.



  • Geoengineering refers to large-scale schemes for intervention in the earthâ€™s oceans, soils and atmosphere with the aim of reducing the effects of climate change. Geoengineering proposals generally fall into two categories: removing carbon from the atmosphere, or shielding Earth from solar radiation.


  • Permaculture is an approach to land management and philosphy that adopts arrangements observed in flourishing natural econsystems. It includes a set of design principles derived using whole systems thinking. It uses these principles in fields such as regneratuve agriculture, rewilding, and community resilience.
